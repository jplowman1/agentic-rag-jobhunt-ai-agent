Clearance: Secret required Rotunda Solutions is a data strategy, analytics, and systems engineering firm providing expertise on high profile projects. We are seeking a highly motivated Senior Technical Advisor and Technical Project Manager to support enterprise AI and autonomy initiatives for a longstanding Department of War client. This role will provide strategic technical leadership and program oversight for AI-enabled autonomy efforts, with a particular focus on computer vision data pipelines, model development infrastructure, and operational deployment capabilities.
The ideal candidate will combine deep technical understanding of AI/ML systems with strong leadership and stakeholder engagement skills, serving as a trusted advisor to senior Department stakeholders while guiding technical teams and capability providers toward mission outcomes. This role requires someone who can operate effectively in complex, multi-stakeholder environments, manage evolving technical requirements, and translate operational needs into executable AI and data engineering initiatives.
The candidate will support Enterprise Frontier AI and Autonomy initiatives, collaborating across Services and Agencies to enable data curation, labeling, training infrastructure, testing and evaluation, and operational model deployment for autonomy-enabled systems.
